# Go-To-Market Plan

## Positioning

"AI receptionist and growth dashboard for Hair Salons, Barbershops, and Beauty Salons."

## Launch Channels

1. Direct outreach (phone/email/social)
2. Local business groups and communities
3. Referral partnerships
4. Organic search and conversion pages

## Funnel

1. Discovery
2. Demo landing experience
3. Trial/demo walkthrough
4. Subscription conversion
5. Onboarding success milestones

## Messaging Themes

- Stop missing bookings
- Respond instantly with AI receptionist
- See revenue and cancellations clearly
- Easy accounting exports

## KPI Targets (Starter)

- Visitor-to-signup conversion %
- Signup-to-paid conversion %
- 30-day retention %
- CAC by channel
