# Full Business Plan

## 1. Vision and Mission

Build the leading AI operating system for Hair Salon, Barbershop, and Beauty Salon businesses, combining booking conversion, service operations, and clear revenue intelligence.

## 2. Product Summary

Core modules:
- Customer discovery and advanced search
- AI receptionist chatbot
- Booking availability and customer booking history
- Subscriber dashboard operations + analytics
- Admin platform analytics and controls
- Accounting integrations and export workflow

## 3. Target Market

Primary customers:
- Independent and small-chain hair/beauty service businesses.

Ideal customer profile:
- 2 to 25 staff
- High call/message volume
- Existing booking friction

## 4. Pricing and Packaging

- Base monthly: GBP 29.99
- Base yearly: GBP 299.99
- Planned tiers:
  - Starter (single location)
  - Growth (advanced automation)
  - Multi-location (enterprise controls)

## 5. Competitive Positioning

Differentiators:
- AI receptionist integrated directly into booking flow
- Unified operations + accounting visibility
- Clean UX for both admin and subscriber roles

## 6. Sales Strategy

- Free live demo and strong onboarding.
- Founder-led initial sales.
- Referral incentives.
- Case-study-driven conversion.

## 7. Marketing Strategy

- Local SEO pages by city/service.
- Short-form educational and social proof content.
- Partner channel outreach to business consultants/accountants.

## 8. Operations Plan

- Weekly release cadence.
- SLA-based support process.
- Monitoring and incident response workflow.
- Roadmap governance and feature rollout controls.

## 9. Technology Plan

- Node/Express backend, PostgreSQL/Prisma
- Role-based dashboard frontend
- Stripe + PayPal billing
- AI integrations with managed guardrails

## 10. Compliance and Legal

Adopt and maintain:
- Terms, privacy, cookies, AUP
- DPA and security policies
- Incident response and breach notification procedures

## 11. Financial Plan (High-Level)

Revenue drivers:
- Subscriber count
- Retention/churn
- Annual prepay ratio

Cost base:
- Infrastructure and API costs
- Support and operations
- Sales/marketing spend

## 12. Key Metrics

- MRR/ARR
- CAC and payback
- Gross churn and net retention
- Conversion by channel
- Booking completion and cancellation rates

## 13. 90-Day Execution Plan

1. Stabilize billing and legal publication
2. Complete onboarding optimization
3. Launch focused acquisition campaigns
4. Implement retention loop (usage nudges + success metrics)
