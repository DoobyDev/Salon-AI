# UK Compliance Checklist (Operational)

This is a practical checklist, not legal advice.

## Corporate and Commercial

- [ ] Company registration details confirmed
- [ ] Registered address and contact details published
- [ ] Terms and Conditions reviewed by solicitor
- [ ] Refund/cancellation policy reviewed

## Data Protection (UK GDPR)

- [ ] Privacy policy finalized
- [ ] Cookie policy/banner setup finalized
- [ ] DPA prepared for subscribers
- [ ] Data retention schedule defined
- [ ] DSAR process documented
- [ ] Breach notification process documented

## Payments and Finance

- [ ] Stripe live setup complete
- [ ] PayPal live setup complete
- [ ] Webhooks verified for both providers
- [ ] Accounting exports tested
- [ ] Tax/VAT position confirmed with accountant

## Security

- [ ] Secret management process
- [ ] Access control process
- [ ] Backup/recovery checks
- [ ] Incident response owner assigned

## Consumer and Marketing

- [ ] Marketing consent wording reviewed
- [ ] Contact preferences respected
- [ ] Business profile accuracy standards set
