# Risk Register

## Risk Scoring

- Impact: Low / Medium / High
- Likelihood: Low / Medium / High

## Register

1. Billing provider outage
- Impact: High
- Likelihood: Medium
- Mitigation: Dual provider support (Stripe + PayPal), retries, status alerts

2. Incorrect AI responses
- Impact: Medium
- Likelihood: Medium
- Mitigation: disclosure, subscriber oversight, UX confirmation steps

3. Data protection non-compliance
- Impact: High
- Likelihood: Medium
- Mitigation: DPA, privacy process, retention controls, legal review

4. Subscriber churn after onboarding
- Impact: High
- Likelihood: Medium
- Mitigation: onboarding checklist, ROI dashboards, support follow-ups

5. Security incident
- Impact: High
- Likelihood: Medium
- Mitigation: access controls, monitoring, incident response process
