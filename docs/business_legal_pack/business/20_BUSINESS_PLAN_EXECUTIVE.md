# Executive Business Plan

## Company

- Name: [PLACEHOLDER]
- Product: AI receptionist, bookings, dashboard analytics, accounting exports.
- Target segments: Hair Salon, Barbershop, Beauty Salon.

## Problem

Service businesses lose revenue from:
- Missed calls and delayed responses
- Booking no-shows/cancellations
- Manual admin workload
- Poor visibility into revenue and retention

## Solution

One platform for:
- Discovery/search and conversion
- AI receptionist and booking workflows
- Subscriber/admin dashboards with live revenue insights
- Accounting export and integrations

## Revenue Model

- Monthly: GBP 29.99
- Yearly: GBP 299.99
- Future upsells: premium analytics, automation packs, multi-location tiers

## Go-To-Market

- Direct outreach to independent salons/shops
- Referral and partner channels
- Local SEO + social proof + demo-led signup funnel

## Milestones (12 Months)

1. Launch and onboarding baseline  
2. Payment reliability and support SLAs  
3. Retention and upsell analytics  
4. Multi-location and advanced automation features

## Risks and Mitigations

- Compliance risk: legal reviews and policy controls
- Churn risk: onboarding and ROI visibility
- Payment risk: dual provider strategy (Stripe + PayPal)
