# Information Security Policy

Effective date: [PLACEHOLDER: DATE]

## 1. Objective

Protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability of platform systems and data.

## 2. Core Controls

- Role-based access control and least privilege.
- Environment variable secret management.
- TLS in transit and secure configuration baselines.
- Logging and audit trails for sensitive events.
- Patch and dependency management processes.

## 3. Access Management

- Unique credentials per user.
- Strong password requirements and secure reset flow.
- Immediate access revocation for terminated users/admins.

## 4. Data Protection

- Minimize personal data collection.
- Restrict production data access to authorized staff only.
- Backup and recovery process with periodic verification.

## 5. Vulnerability and Incident Process

- Track vulnerabilities and remediation SLA by severity.
- Maintain incident response runbook.

## 6. Vendor Security

Third-party providers are selected with security/compliance due diligence.
