## 1. Overview

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal data across the platform.

## 2. Categories of Data

- Identity/contact: name, email, phone.
- Account data: role, login details, authentication metadata.
- Booking data: appointment details, preferences, notes.
- Business data: salon profile, services, operating hours.
- Technical data: IP, device, logs, analytics events.
- Billing data: subscription status and provider identifiers (Stripe/PayPal).

We do not intentionally collect special category data unless explicitly required and lawfully handled.

## 3. Sources

- Directly from users.
- From subscriber businesses.
- From integrated processors (Stripe, PayPal, Twilio, SendGrid, cloud/hosting).

## 4. Purposes and Legal Bases

- Service delivery and account administration: contract.
- Billing and fraud prevention: contract and legitimate interests.
- Product analytics and improvements: legitimate interests.
- Legal compliance (tax/accounting/regulatory): legal obligation.
- Marketing communications (where applicable): consent or legitimate interests (as permitted).

## 5. AI Processing

AI assistant interactions may be processed to provide chat and booking assistance.  
Do not submit highly sensitive personal data in chat fields.

## 6. Sharing

Data may be shared with:
- Infrastructure and payment processors.
- Notification providers.
- Professional advisors and authorities where legally required.

No sale of personal data.

## 7. International Transfers

Where transfers occur outside the UK, we use lawful transfer mechanisms (e.g., adequacy, SCCs/IDTA, vendor safeguards).

## 8. Retention

- Account data: for active service term + lawful retention period.
- Booking/audit/accounting records: retained according to legal/accounting requirements.
- Security logs: retained based on operational/security necessity.

## 9. Security

We apply technical and organizational controls including access control, environment segregation, and logging.
