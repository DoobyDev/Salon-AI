## 1. AI Feature Scope

The AI receptionist assists with:
- General service information
- Booking assistance
- Basic support prompts

## 2. Limitations

AI outputs may be inaccurate or incomplete.  
Subscribers must validate critical details (pricing, availability, policies).

## 3. No Professional Advice

AI responses are not legal, financial, tax, medical, or other regulated advice.

## 4. Human Override

Subscribers remain responsible for final customer communications and booking outcomes.

## 5. Prohibited AI Use

No use for:
- Harmful instructions
- Discrimination
- Defamation
- Privacy-invasive profiling
- Illegal content generation
