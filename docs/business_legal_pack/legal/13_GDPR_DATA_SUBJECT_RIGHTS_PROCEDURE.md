# GDPR Data Subject Rights Procedure (DSAR / Requests)

Effective date: [PLACEHOLDER: DATE]

## 1. Scope

Procedure for handling requests relating to:
- Access
- Rectification
- Erasure
- Restriction
- Portability
- Objection
- Consent withdrawal

## 2. Intake Channels

Requests may be received via:
- Privacy email: [PLACEHOLDER]
- Support ticket
- In-app request form (if available)

## 3. Identity Verification

Verify requester identity before disclosing personal data.

## 4. Logging

Track each request:
- Request ID
- Date received
- Request type
- Identity verification status
- Deadline
- Outcome

## 5. Response Time

Target: respond within applicable legal deadline (e.g., one month under UK GDPR), subject to lawful extensions.

## 6. Grounds to Refuse / Limit

Assess whether refusal or limitation is lawful (e.g., manifestly unfounded/excessive requests, rights of others, legal obligations).

## 7. Fulfilment Steps

1. Identify systems and processors holding relevant data.
2. Collect and review data.
3. Apply redactions if needed.
4. Deliver response securely.
5. Record completion and rationale.

## 8. Escalation

Escalate complex or high-risk requests to legal/compliance owner.
