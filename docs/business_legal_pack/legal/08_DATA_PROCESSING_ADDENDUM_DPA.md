# Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Effective date: [PLACEHOLDER: DATE]

This DPA supplements the Terms between:
- Controller/Customer: Subscriber business
- Processor: [PLACEHOLDER: LEGAL ENTITY NAME]

## 1. Subject Matter

Processing of personal data to provide booking, communication, analytics, and platform operations.

## 2. Duration

For the term of the subscriber agreement, plus any required retention period.

## 3. Nature and Purpose

- Hosting and account management
- Booking and appointment workflow
- Messaging notifications
- Usage analytics and security monitoring

## 4. Data Types and Data Subjects

Data subjects:
- Subscriber staff/users
- Customer end users

Data types:
- Contact and account details
- Booking and communication data
- Technical identifiers and logs

## 5. Processor Obligations

Processor will:
- Process only on documented instructions.
- Ensure confidentiality obligations.
- Implement appropriate security controls.
- Support controller with data subject rights requests.
- Notify controller of personal data breaches without undue delay.

## 6. Sub-Processors

Processor may use vetted sub-processors (e.g., hosting, payment, messaging providers).  
Processor remains responsible for sub-processor compliance.

## 7. International Transfers

Transfers will use lawful mechanisms (adequacy, SCCs/IDTA, or equivalent).

## 8. Deletion or Return

On termination, data is deleted or returned subject to legal retention obligations.

## 9. Audit and Evidence

Processor will provide reasonable compliance information and evidence on request.
