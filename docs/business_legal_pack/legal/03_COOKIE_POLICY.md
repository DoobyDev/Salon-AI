## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device to help websites function and improve user experience.

## 2. Cookie Types We Use

- Functional: preferences and UX settings.
- Analytics: usage and performance insights.

## 3. Legal Basis

- Necessary cookies: legitimate interests / service necessity.
- Non-essential cookies: consent where required.

## 4. Managing Cookies

Users can:
- Adjust cookie settings in-browser.
- Clear cookies at any time.
- Manage consent via cookie banner/settings (if enabled).

Third-party providers may set cookies under their own policies.

## 6. Updates

Cookie practices may change; latest version will be published in-app/site.
