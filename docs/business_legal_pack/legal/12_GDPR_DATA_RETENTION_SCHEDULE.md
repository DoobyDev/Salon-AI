## Retention Principles

- Retain only what is necessary.
- Define clear business/legal reasons for retention.
- Delete or anonymize data when no longer needed.
- Apply legal hold exceptions where required.

## Disposal Method

- Secure deletion from active systems

- Anonymization where analytics value remains
