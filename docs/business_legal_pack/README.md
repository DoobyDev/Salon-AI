# Business + Legal Document Pack

This folder contains a complete starter document set for the Salon AI app.

Important:
- These are professional templates, not legal advice.
- You should have a qualified solicitor/law firm review before publishing or signing.

## Pack Structure

- `00_MASTER_INDEX.md`: master index and document map.
- `PRINTING_INSTRUCTIONS.md`: how to print each document.
- `legal/`: legal policies and agreements.
- `business/`: business plan and operating documents.
- `print/`: print-ready consolidated HTML files.

## Included GDPR + Security Coverage

- UK GDPR privacy policy template
- DPA template
- DSAR/data subject rights procedure
- Data retention schedule
- RoPA template
- DPIA template
- Sub-processor register template
- Information security policy
- Incident response / breach notification policy
- Access control and password policy
- Backup / business continuity policy
- Vulnerability and patch management policy

## Required Before Use

1. Replace every `[PLACEHOLDER: ...]` field.
2. Add your registered business details.
3. Confirm jurisdictions and tax language.
4. Run legal review and approve final redlines.
